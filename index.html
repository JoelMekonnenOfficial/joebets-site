No. The value is in the picks themselves, not a 7-day demo. If it's not for you after a month, cancel in one click.

Combat sports are the core: UFC and boxing specifically. NBA and NFL when the edge is there. Plus a standing prediction-markets section for Polymarket and Kalshi plays.

Volume is whatever the board gives. Some days four or five plays. Some days one. Sometimes zero. Patience is the whole game.
